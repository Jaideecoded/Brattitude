About Alex

The artist behind the lashes

Hi, I'm Alex

Based in Chelsea, London, I'm a passionate lash artist with a refined eye for beauty and detail. My journey in lash artistry began with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and pampered.

I specialize in creating bespoke lash extensions that are meticulously tailored to complement each client's unique eye shape, facial features, and personal style.

"My philosophy is simple: enhance natural beauty with precision, care, and artistry. Each set of lashes I create is a work of art, designed to make you feel like the best version of yourself."
