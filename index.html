Hi, I'm Alex

Originally from Leicester and now based in Chelsea, London, I'm a passionate lash artist with a refined eye for beauty and detail. My journey in lash artistry began with a simple belief: that everyone deserves to feel confident, beautiful, and pampered.

I specialize in creating bespoke lash extensions that are meticulously tailored to complement each client's unique eye shape, facial features, and personal style. My approach combines technical precision with artistic vision to deliver results that are both stunning and harmonious.

"My philosophy is simple: enhance natural beauty with precision, care, and artistry. Each set of lashes I create is a work of art, designed to make you feel like the best version of yourself."
